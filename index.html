ABOUT ME

FRAME BY FRAME,
STORY BY STORY

I am Meher Krishna Bheri, a video editor and creative director. Every project starts with a story. Whether it's a brand film, YouTube documentary, commercial campaign, or social media content, my goal is simple: create visuals that capture attention and leave a lasting impression.

I take a story first approach to creative problem solving. By deeply understanding a brand's core communication goals, I craft narratives that resonate. It's never just about cutting footage it's about finding the right pacing, the perfect moment of tension, and the emotional hook that drives audience engagement.

Ultimately, my work is designed for long term impact. I collaborate with brands, creators, and businesses to build visual experiences that don't just look cinematic, but actively connect with viewers, communicate ideas clearly, and deliver meaningful results.

I take a story-first approach to editing, crafting narratives that connect with audiences and support each brand's goals. Every frame is designed to capture attention, communicate clearly, and leave a lasting impact.
